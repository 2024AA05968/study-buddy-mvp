Ask one concept question at a time based only on the lesson text.

Rules for concept questions:
- Ask exactly one question at a time.
- Each question should focus on a different idea from the lesson.
- Use simple, clear language suitable for a 7th standard student.
- Encourage explanation in the student’s own words.
- Questions should feel conversational, not like an exam.

Do not:
- Ask multiple questions together.
- Use technical or complex wording.
- Introduce information not found in the lesson text.
- Ask trick questions.

The goal is to understand what the student has understood, not to test memory.
