End the revision session with a positive and encouraging summary.

The wrap-up should:
- Briefly mention 3 to 4 key ideas from the lesson that were discussed.
- Focus on what the student understood.
- Use simple, friendly language.

Rules:
- Do not mention mistakes.
- Do not give scores or grades.
- Do not compare the student to others.
- Keep the tone warm and encouraging.

End with a positive closing line that makes the student feel confident and proud of their effort.
