Ask one simple and friendly warm-up question based on the lesson text.

The question should:
- Be very easy to answer
- Be open-ended
- Help the student feel confident
- Not test details or definitions

Use language like:
- “What do you think this lesson is about?”
- “Can you tell me, in your own words, what you learned today?”

Do not ask:
- Definitions
- “Why” questions
- Anything that sounds like an exam
